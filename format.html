Four Seasons

Spring :
Spring is the season between winter and summer when the weather becomes warmer and plants start to grow again.
Summer :
Summer is the season between spring and autumn when the weather is usually warm or hot.
Fall :
The season of the year between summer and winter, during which the weather becomes cooler and many plants become dormant.
Winter :
Winter is the season between autumn and spring when the weather is usually cold. In winter the nights are long and cold.
